Services

The Hospital was inaugurated on 7th April 2008 under the able leadership of Rt. Rev. Dr. Thomas A Vazapilly, the Bishop of Mysore, who is also the President of the St. Joseph’s Hospital Trust. The Diocese of Mysore started this hospital with the avowed aim of providing quality health care at an affordable cost irrespective of caste, creed and religion to all patients. Situated at Bannimantap, on the Mysore-Bangalore highway on a 33 acres of spacious land, the Hospital building is surrounded by greenery providing an echo-friendly look. The hospital building is planned in such a way that every room and corridor is blessed with natural light and cross ventilation to every part of the building.
