Instructions for Applicants of Health Card:
  • Kindly read the terms and conditions before filling the application form.
  • Please fill in the application form completely in all respects.
  • Please write the complete name (including the names of dependents in case of family scheme) and address legibly.
Annual Access Fee
  • Rs. 250/-  for individual scheme.
  • Under family scheme Rs. 250/-  for primary card holder and Rs. 125/- for every additional member. Maximum 8 members can be covered in one card.
  • For 3 years-10% discount and for 5 years-20% discount will be offered
Validity of the Card
  • The validity of the Health Card is for one year from the date of registration.
  • Once a duly completed application along with annual fee is received, no request for cancellation / transfer / refund will be entertained.
Services to Members
  • Only the primary card holder and in the event of coverage under the family scheme, the family of the primary card holder is authorised to use the services of the hospital. In the event of misuse of the card, the membership to the scheme is liable to be cancelled and no refund will be made.
